Deadside (Video Game)


NPC(Non Player Character) are the second biggest threat in this game. There are different grades of NPC's with different weaopons and different strenght levels. They appear on 3 different occasions.

Occasion 1: Randomly partloing the streets. These are bots that roam in smaller groups(3-7) usually they stick to raods or buildings.There are 2 different versions of them: "low gear" and "military gear" Whilest the low geard opose no real threat, the military ones do. But with a little experience neither one should be a problem.


Occasion 2: Guarding the Missions. These bots vary from mission grade, whilest grade easy and medium are easy to beat, the rest can be a bit tricky.


Occasion 3: Guarding the Helicopter-crashsite. These bots are a major threat and not to be toyed around with. They are geard with automatic rifles, usually Ak's. Your best and only choice is to take them out with a Sniper, but even this is no simply task. Since these bots shoot with a super high accurarcy.
