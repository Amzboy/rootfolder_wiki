Base Building


Basebuilding in Deadside isn't the most popular activity to do. Compared to other games Rust BaseBuilding is very unpopular. This has mulitple reasons.

First of all it takes a lot of affort to actually start building a base. First of all you need a Hammer, Saw and Axe to actually start building. To find all of those items in the same life, can alone be a challange to some. Besides the mentioned tools you also need a ton of nails to actually start Building a decent base(1000+). So one way or another you will end up in the safezone buying those said things.

Now there comes the second challange: Finding a place to build your base. As of now, the places you can build in are very limited, since they can't be too close to raods, structures, safezones, or other players bases.

Setting a home spawn point or any respawn point is not possible at the time. Also raiding is not included yet, tho the dev team has said that raiding is a feature to come.

How to actually build a base?


Step 1: Craft yourself a "Land Claim" and run around the map and wait until it flashes from red to green. Keep in mind that you can't build too close to roads, structures, safezone or enemy bases.



Step 2: Use your Axe to farm wood(hit trees with), make sure you have inventory space. Once you have farmed wood, you will be able to convert wooden logs into wooden planks, beams, plywood(These you will need in order to craft further constructions).


Step 3: With your now farmed wood and nails, you can craft various constructions. From roofs and walls to foundations and doors.


Step 4: Now select your crafted constructions inventory and construct them with holding down the F key.


Step 5: The last step is to put a electronic lock(4 digit lock) on your door, so only allies can come inside. This item can either be bought at the Safzone for 100k rubel or crafted.
