Deadside (Video Game)


Deadside is a hardcore first person only multiplayer-only survival video game developed by Bad Pixel. Deadside was first released in early access in April 2020. Deadside is available on Microsoft Windows. Console versions and Mac Ios hasn’t been confirmed yet, but Bad Pixel hasn’t ruled anything out.

The objective of Deadside is to survive and compete in the wilderness using looted or stolen materials/supplies. As of now the primary threat is other players due to the game being solely multiplayer. In addition, non-player characters(called scarfs), armed with lighter guns, roam in smaller groups(3+) around the map. Scarfs can be distinguished from players by their bad aim and movement. Deadside features crafting. To protect you and your hard farmed loot, players must build bases or save their hard earned goods in their sotrage in the Safezone. Storage is limited to 16 slots, unless the Deadsie Supporter Pack has been purchased, then the storage is limited to 34 Slots.

Throughout Deadsides lifetime, critical reviews are mixed, with many comparisons made to other survival games. Deadside is often described as a mixture of DayZ, H1Z1, Rust and Escape from Tarkov. For the time beeing reviewers frequently noted the game's unfinished nature and lack of content. The majority of the reviews of the game are postiv, it is often noted that it is really well polished for an early acces game. 8/10 reviews are postitiv.
