Mission/Events


are one of the major parts of the game. Missions Drop Loot depending on their difficulty level. So whilest you are almost garanteed to not get much more then 25k ruble in an easy misson, you can expect a good gun or other rare item in hard or epic missions.


These are the different Mission level, which are shown on the map, from easy to epic:



Helicopter-crashe's


will apear all around the map. They contain high tier loot but are also very well guarded. There are around 8 Ak NPC's with a very high accuracy guarding the crashsite. A common strategy is to play it long distance with sniper rifles.



Airdrop's


will apear all around the map. They can contain high tier loot and are not guarded. So your main threat are other players, which are potentially camping at the Airdrop.
