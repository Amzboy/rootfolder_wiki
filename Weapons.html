Weapons


Arguably the most important part of this game. There are 5 different catagories of weapons. Them beeing: Melee, Handgun, Shotgun, Rifel and automatic Rifel. Whilst automatic Rifels are Military grade only. Which means that they can only be optained by looting military camps or killing military Scarfs.

Melee Weapons




Handguns


Can be carried as a secondary gun.



Shotguns


Are rifles which shoot 12x70 Gauge Buckshot ammo. Shotguns are well suitet for closecombat gameplay. Depending on the range and accuracy of the shot, shotguns can do from 2 to up to 100 Damage.



Automatic Rifles


Arguably the most popular kind of kinds. Overall they are fairly rare, and can only be found in military scarfs or at military camps.



Rifles


Are large caliber guns, which are suited for long rang fights. Projectiles of such guns do i high amount of damage but therefor can only be shot very slowley.
