Gameplay


As a multiplayer-only video game, Deadside pits players against each other in a harsh, open world environment with the sole goal of survival. Scarfs act as a looming threat, but the primary danger comes from other players. Gameplay takes place a pre-built map. Player vs player (PvP) combat is mainly accomplished with guns, also in some occasions melee weapons are also an viable option. Bullets and other projectiles travel in a ballistic trajectory, rather than being hitscan. There are a number of different guns and ammo types. Hit tracking calculates damage: shots to the head are more damaging than shots to other parts of the body. The use of weapon attachments, such as weapon sights and flashlights, provides an advantage over opponents. To survive, the player must loot, craft and team with other players. Building bases is'nt a necesscry used strategy. People who rather stay on the move, store their most valuable loot, in their privat lockers, which are located in one of the 2 safezone's.

As stated above the main combat in this game is acomplished by gun-pvp. Different kinds of guns are effective on different kind of ranges.(As showen in Weapons). The maximum render distance is 299 Meter and with the longest range you can possible kill someone is also 299m.
