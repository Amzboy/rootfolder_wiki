Safezone


There are 2 safezones on the map. They are marked with a big green circle. It is not possible to take damagen from other players in the safezone.



Almost any item is tradeable with the 2 friendly NPC's there. Tho it should be noted that when you are selling they will only pay 1/5 of the price for what they are selling it for. Greedy rats.
